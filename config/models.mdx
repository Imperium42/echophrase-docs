# Model Selection

Echophrase uses OpenAI's Whisper models. Choose the right one for your needs.

## Available Models

| Model | Size | Speed | Accuracy | Best For |
|-------|------|-------|----------|----------|
| **Tiny** | ~75 MB | Fastest | Good | Quick notes, low-end hardware |
| **Base** | ~150 MB | Fast | Better | Daily use, balanced |
| **Small** | ~500 MB | Medium | Great | Professional use |
| **Medium** | ~1.5 GB | Slower | Excellent | High accuracy needs |
| **Large** | ~3 GB | Slowest | Best | Maximum accuracy |

## Recommendations

- **CPU only**: Start with Tiny or Base
- **NVIDIA GPU**: Base or Small for best balance
- **High-end GPU**: Medium or Large for maximum accuracy

## Changing Models

1. Open Echophrase settings
2. Navigate to **AI Model**
3. Select your preferred model
4. Wait for the download to complete (~1-2 minutes)

<Note>
Models are downloaded once and cached locally. Switching between downloaded models is instant.
</Note>
