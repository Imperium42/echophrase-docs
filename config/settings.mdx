# Settings

Customize Echophrase to fit your workflow.

## General Settings

- **Start on boot**: Launch Echophrase when your computer starts
- **Minimize to tray**: Keep Echophrase running in the system tray
- **Sound feedback**: Play sounds when recording starts/stops

## Output Settings

- **Auto-punctuation**: Automatically add punctuation to transcriptions
- **Auto-capitalize**: Capitalize the first letter of sentences
- **Typing speed**: Control how fast text is typed out

## Audio Settings

- **Input device**: Select your microphone
- **Noise suppression**: Filter out background noise
- **Voice activity detection**: Automatically detect when you stop speaking
